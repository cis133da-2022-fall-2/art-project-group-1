1917: Review on One-shot Style!

The film 1917 makes excellent use of the continous or one-shot style of filming. The style was used to great effect and made the entire film feel as if it were all one shot, despite being multiple long shots edited together in post. The use of this style really adds to the emotional connection to the film and its characters. It allows the viewer to feel as if they are right there In the moment with the characters experiencing what they are along side them.

While this film does make excellant use of the one-shot style of filming it is far from the only example of such and many other pieces of media that use one-shot to great effect.
